Dr. Alan Gaby is a pioneering physician who has spent over four decades advancing the field of nutritional medicine through rigorous research, comprehensive education, and clinical excellence. His monumental textbook "Nutritional Medicine" has become the definitive reference for healthcare practitioners worldwide, establishing evidence-based protocols for using nutrients as therapeutic agents.
