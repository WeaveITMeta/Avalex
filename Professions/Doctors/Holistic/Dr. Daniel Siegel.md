## Overview

Dr. Daniel Siegel is a world-renowned psychiatrist, neuroscientist, and author who has transformed our understanding of the mind, brain, and relationships through his groundbreaking work in interpersonal neurobiology. As a clinical professor of psychiatry at UCLA and founding co-director of the Mindful Awareness Research Center, he has pioneered the integration of neuroscience with mindfulness practices, creating innovative approaches to mental health, parenting, and education.
