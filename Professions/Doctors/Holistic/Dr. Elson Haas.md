## Overview

Dr. Elson Haas is a pioneering integrative medicine physician who has spent over four decades transforming healthcare through his innovative approaches to preventive medicine, detoxification, and seasonal health. As the founder and director of the Preventive Medical Center of Marin, he has developed comprehensive protocols that help patients achieve optimal health through nutrition, lifestyle changes, and natural therapeutics.
