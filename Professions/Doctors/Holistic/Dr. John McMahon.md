## Overview

The late Dr. John McMahon was a visionary cardiologist who pioneered the integration of holistic approaches with conventional cardiology. His groundbreaking work in integrative cardiology transformed how heart disease is understood and treated, emphasizing the interconnection between emotional well-being, lifestyle factors, and cardiovascular health. His legacy continues to influence modern integrative cardiac care.
