# Dr. Kelly Brogan

## Professional Profile

**Specialty**: Psychiatry, Holistic Mental Health

## Overview

Dr. Kelly Brogan is a pioneering holistic psychiatrist who has transformed the landscape of mental health treatment by advocating for root-cause resolution rather than symptom suppression. With training from MIT and Cornell, she has developed comprehensive protocols that address mental health through nutrition, lifestyle modification, and mind-body practices, challenging conventional psychiatric paradigms and empowering patients to reclaim their mental wellness.

## Treatment Philosophy

"Mental illness is not a genetic destiny or chemical imbalance. It's a symptom of imbalance in the body that can be addressed through nutrition, detoxification, lifestyle changes, and psychological healing. True healing comes from addressing root causes, not suppressing symptoms."
