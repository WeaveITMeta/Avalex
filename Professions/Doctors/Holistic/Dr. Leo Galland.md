## Overview

Dr. Leo Galland is a board-certified internist and internationally recognized pioneer in functional medicine who has transformed the practice of medicine through his patient-centered diagnostic approach. With decades of experience treating complex chronic illnesses, he has developed innovative frameworks for understanding the unique biochemical individuality of each patient and creating personalized treatment protocols.
