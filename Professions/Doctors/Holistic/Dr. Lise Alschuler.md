# Dr. Lise Alschuler

## Professional Profile

**Specialty**: Naturopathic Oncology, Integrative Cancer Care

## Overview

Dr. Lise Alschuler is a leading naturopathic oncologist who has dedicated her career to advancing integrative cancer care. As a cancer survivor herself, she brings both professional expertise and personal insight to her work, helping patients navigate cancer treatment while optimizing their overall health and quality of life. Her evidence-based approach combines the best of naturopathic medicine with conventional oncology.
