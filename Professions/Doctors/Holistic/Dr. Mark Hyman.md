## Professional Profile

**Specialty**: Functional Medicine, Weight Management

## Overview

Dr. Mark Hyman is one of the world's leading authorities on functional medicine, having dedicated his career to transforming healthcare through a systems-based approach that addresses the root causes of chronic disease. As a 14-time New York Times bestselling author and director of prestigious medical centers, he has been instrumental in bringing functional medicine into mainstream healthcare while advocating for food as medicine and comprehensive lifestyle interventions.
