# Dr. Penelope Ody

## Professional Profile

**Specialty**: Herbal Medicine, Eclectic Herbalism  
**Known For**: Author of "The Complete Book of Herbs and Herbal Medicine"  
**Legacy**: Founder of the Council of Bachelors and Bachelors of Botanical Medicine

## Overview

Dr. Penelope Ody is a renowned herbalist, author, and educator who has significantly influenced modern herbal medicine practice. As the author of numerous definitive texts on herbal medicine and founder of important professional organizations, she has helped establish standards for herbal education and practice while making traditional plant medicine accessible to both practitioners and the public.
