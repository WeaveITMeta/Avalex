## Overview

Dr. Tieraona Low Dog is an internationally recognized expert in integrative medicine, natural health, and herbal medicine. With a unique background that bridges traditional indigenous healing practices with modern medical science, she has become one of the most trusted voices in botanical medicine and women's health. Her work has influenced medical education, clinical practice, and public health policy worldwide.
